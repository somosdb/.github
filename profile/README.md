At DB, we believe that sharing knowledge is one of the most effective ways to grow as a team, connect our different areas, and contribute to the community. We do this by opening our work to review, collaboration, and the exchange of experiences, while also publishing tools, research, and projects developed by our teams.

DB employees retain ownership of their projects and the corresponding intellectual property rights. As a result, many of the initiatives supported by DB are distributed across individual profiles and different organizations on GitHub. We have brought these projects together here to connect initiatives from across DB’s various areas and make them easier to find and follow.

You can learn more about our work and services at [db.tec.br](https://db.tec.br).
